RI serires

The Centurion RI Series stands as the pinnacle of wake sports boats, offering unparalleled performance and luxury. Each RI Series model is equipped with the Opti-V hull design that ensures a consistent and clean wave for wakeboarding and wakesurfing enthusiasts. The RI Series is further enhanced by the RAMFILL ballast system, which provides rapid and substantial wave customization, allowing for a tailored wake sports experience. Sophisticated interiors and advanced technology, including touchscreen controls and premium sound systems, make the RI Series a top choice for those who prioritize comfort and performance on the water.

FI series

Centurion's FI Series boats are tailored for both serious athletes and recreational enthusiasts who seek a perfect blend of performance and versatility. The FI Series features a modified Deep-V Hull design that offers exceptional handling and a smooth ride, even in choppy waters. These boats are equipped with the CATS system, allowing for precise adjustments of the wake's shape, making it ideal for both surfing and wakeboarding. With spacious layouts and flexible seating configurations, the FI Series boats are not only performance-driven but also perfect for entertaining and relaxing.

FE series

The Centurion FE Series is designed for efficiency and ease of use, making it an excellent choice for families and entry-level enthusiasts. These boats come with a user-friendly setup that includes basic wake-enhancing technologies and a comfortable, durable interior. The FE Series offers a straightforward approach to wake sports, providing just enough customization for novice riders to enjoy a safe and enjoyable introduction to the sport. With an emphasis on value and simplicity, the FE Series delivers reliable performance and fun on the water without the complexity of the higher-end models.
